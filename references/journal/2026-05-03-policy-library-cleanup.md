# Policy library cleanup

Date: 2026-05-03

## Change

Renamed the awkward "Informative layer / Fundamentals" tab to "Policy library."

## Structure

The learning side now has a clearer hierarchy:

- Orientation cards for how to read the policy library.
- Legislation and statutory anchors as the main reader.
- Core principles for reading the policy universe.
- A separate Reference tab for sources, policy mechanisms, timeline, and glossary.

## Legislation reader

Each statutory anchor now has:

- A short sidebar blurb.
- A main explainer.
- Policy mechanics.
- Key provisions or concepts to understand.
- Connected policy areas.
- Watch next.
- Primary source links.

The goal is to make each item useful for someone who already follows the topic but wants a durable mental model.
