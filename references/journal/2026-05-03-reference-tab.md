# Reference tab

Date: 2026-05-03

## Change

Added a third top-level tab: Reference.

## Rationale

The app now separates three reader intents:

- Latest federal watch: what changed recently.
- Policy library: what the laws and frameworks mean.
- Reference: lookup material that supports both modes.

## Moved into Reference

- Sources to monitor.
- Policy mechanism map.
- Implementation timeline.
- Glossary.

## Kept in Policy library

- Orientation cards.
- Legislation and statutory anchors.
- Core principles for reading the policy universe.
