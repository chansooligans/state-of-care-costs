# Tabs and broader news source universe

Date: 2026-05-03

## Product change

The app now separates the two core jobs:

- Latest federal watch: a current-events surface for informed readers.
- Policy library: durable background for understanding the policy universe.
- Reference: lookup material for sources, mechanisms, timeline, and glossary terms.

This prevents latest news from crowding out fundamentals, and prevents evergreen explainers from feeling stale inside a news feed.

## News universe

The latest watch should not be CMS-only. CMS remains central, but federal cost and transparency policy also moves through HHS, DOL EBSA, FTC, the Federal Register, Congress.gov, the White House, and HHS OIG. Outside primary sources, the tracker can include research and journalism when they clarify implementation, data quality, stakeholder conflict, or real-world use.

Initial non-CMS source categories:

- Federal: HHS, DOL EBSA, FTC, White House, Federal Register, Congress.gov, HHS OIG.
- Research: Peterson-KFF, KFF, AJMC.
- Journalism: KFF Health News, Axios.
- Industry/stakeholder: AHA and other stakeholder sources, clearly labeled.

## Editorial rules

- Label source type visibly.
- Prefer primary sources for what happened.
- Use research and journalism for why it matters and whether the policy is working.
- Keep state policy out of scope for now.
- Track proposals separately from enacted law and final rules.
- Do not treat executive orders, proposed rules, final rules, statutes, research papers, and news reporting as interchangeable.
